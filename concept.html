Fees

Designed to be simple and transparent.

OVÉ is being explored with a simple annual fee structure. Final structure depends on the regulated partner. No entry fees are currently expected. We aim for full fee transparency.

How investing has behaved historically

Over long periods, stock and bond markets have delivered a wide range of annual returns. These are not predictions — just historical context to help understand how outcomes can vary.

Lower-risk mix ~3% – 6% annual returns
Mixed stocks and funds ~5% – 8% annual returns
Higher-risk mix ~7% – 10% annual returns

These ranges are based on historical market data across different asset mixes and time periods. They do not represent any OVÉ product or expected outcome. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. Returns have varied significantly across different time periods, including negative outcomes. Capital is at risk.

OVÉ is not currently authorised to provide investment services. No authorised partner is currently engaged. Any future regulated service would depend on a signed arrangement with an authorised firm and any applicable approvals.
