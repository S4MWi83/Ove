What we're building

Built for the 91% who haven't started.

1

A pre-launch investing platform.

Ové is designed to get complete beginners invested — from €10, in under two minutes, with no unnecessary choices and no jargon.

2

For the person who hasn't started yet.

Not for traders or experienced investors. For people who know they should be investing but have been put off by friction, jargon, or the fear of getting it wrong.

3

Calm, simple, and regulated.

No countdown timers. No FOMO. No behavioural nudges. Ové will operate through a licensed partner under MiFID II — compliance is a design requirement, not an afterthought.
