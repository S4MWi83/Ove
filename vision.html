Target User

The barrier is not money or motivation. It's friction — unnecessary terms, too many choices, and a fear of making a costly mistake.

Ové is structured around a clear progression from first-time investor to fully automated portfolio.

Vision

From someday to today. From today to always.

The target user is the European who intends to invest but hasn't started — not the active trader or the financial professional. The goal is to get them started, keep them consistent, and ultimately hand off portfolio management entirely to automation.

Ové is designed as a long-term product: a clear path from a first €10 investment — in the intended product experience — to a fully managed portfolio via Ové Advisor.

What Ové is not:

  • Not a bank
  • Not a trading platform
  • Not for short-term speculation
  • Not built for experienced traders
  • Not trying to be everything to everyone
  • Not currently authorised to provide investment services

How returns are calculated

Illustrative, not predictive.

The return ranges shown in Ové's portfolios are based on long-term historical index performance — not forecasts or predictions. Here's what that means in practice:

  • Figures are drawn from historical average annual returns across different market conditions
  • They do not account for fees, taxes, or individual market timing
  • Actual outcomes may be significantly different, including negative returns over extended periods
  • Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results
  • Capital is at risk — investors may lose some or all of their invested capital

These scenarios do not follow the PRIIPs Key Information Document (KID) methodology and are provided for illustrative purposes only. A full KID will be produced prior to any investment being made available.

Go-to-Market

Where we start.

Initial user acquisition via paid social and creator partnerships in Sweden, the Netherlands, and the UK — English-comfortable markets with low investment participation. Secondary focus on urban 18–40 audiences in Italy and Spain, where the opportunity is largest.

Regulation

Built to launch without a standalone licence.

Ové is designed to operate through a licensed brokerage partner under MiFID IIMiFID IIEU law that regulates investment services — it exists to protect you as an investor.. Regulatory and compliance infrastructure will be provided at the partner level — Ové does not require a standalone licence to launch.

Any future investment service will be subject to appropriateness and/or suitability assessments as required under MiFID II, ensuring the product is matched to each user's knowledge, experience, and financial situation before they invest.

Regulation is core to Ové. Compliance is treated as a starting point for design, shaping how products, communication, and user interactions are built from the ground up.

Waitlist members may be notified as Ové progresses toward launch. Subject to a confirmed brokerage partner arrangement and applicable regulatory approvals, the option to begin account setup may be made available to waitlist members ahead of any public launch.
