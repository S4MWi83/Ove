What Has Inflation Done to Your Money?

Using real EU27 HICP data from Eurostat, 2015–2025.

What this calculator does

This calculator uses published EU27 HICP annual average inflation data from Eurostat to show how the purchasing power of a savings amount has changed over a chosen time period. Calculations apply each year's published annual average rate sequentially, working backwards from 2025. No projections, no forward-looking estimates, no investment advice. Historical data only.
